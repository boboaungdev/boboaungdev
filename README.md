## Building

### [StratArena](https://stratarena.com)

A crypto strategy backtesting app for testing, refining, and comparing trading ideas in a clearer workflow.

The goal is to make strategy building feel more structured and less chaotic, so traders can move from an idea to a testable workflow with more clarity.

I want it to feel:

- clear before it feels powerful
- fast before it feels complex
- reliable before it feels clever

Right now I am focused on:

- shaping a calmer backtesting workflow
- making strategy logic easier to understand and review
- building product foundations that can scale into a larger platform

---

## What I Work On

I build software that needs to work beyond the landing page.

- Mobile apps with Expo and React Native
- Node.js backends with clean modular structure
- Real-time systems using Socket.IO, WebRTC, and Redis
- Auth, notifications, and background flows that need to hold up in production

What I care about most:

- architecture that stays understandable as a product grows
- flows that remain reliable across devices and unstable networks
- interfaces that feel focused instead of overloaded
- systems that are easier to maintain than they are to impress with
